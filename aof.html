Rare diseases in the
middle east

We focus on breakthrough medicines to help patients improve their lives. Our mission is to provide innovative treatments for patients with unmet medical needs in the Middle East and Africa through partnerships. Therefore, we are selectively active in the areas of rare, orphan, speciality and targeted medicines. We have one of the most diverse portfolios for hard to treat diseases By following innovative science, we hope to achieve life-changing benefits for patients in our region.

Cystic fibrosis

We are proud of our commitment to treat Cystic Fibrosis in our region. CF is a rare, life-threatening genetic disease caused by mutations of the cystic fibrosis transmembrane conductance regulator – or CFTR – gene.In the Middle East, the prevalence is estimated at 1 in 30,000-50,000 while the incidence is estimated at 1-2000-5800 live births. we are working to give patients more ways to treat CF, more quality in their lives & more time. we have partnered with Vertex, the leading comapny in creating transformative medicines for CF patients
